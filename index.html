My Journey

Musical Beginnings (2012-2015)

2012 - 2015

I was never the best in school, but I always had a love for the arts. One of my first accomplishments was performing excellently on trumpet and in band overall. Our marching band, jazz band, and concert band all won many competitions with me involved.

I made it to the district jazz and concert band, and played in the Pearl Harbor Memorial Parade in 2014. At the young age of 12-15, this endeavor required a serious amount of dedication and practice.

Business & Entrepreneurship (2017-2020)

2017 - 2020

As I grew older, my ambition started to grow a little big for my shoes. I decided that I needed to raise capital to become a business owner. Over these years, I learned valuable lessons about risk management, logistics, and business operations.

During this time, I also became a local musician, teaching myself how to make music and performing multiple local shows. I even sold out one of the more popular venues in Asheville, NC where I grew up.

Personal Growth & Skill Development (2020-2024)

2020 - 2024

I started becoming a chef working in local kitchens. This time wasn't wasted one bit. Everyone is the Chef of their own kitchen in life; long nights and doubles in the kitchen taught me patience, resilience, and most importantly how to be efficient.

In my free time, I taught myself everything about FL Studio Digital Audio Workspace (DAW). I tried my hand at TikTok and promoted my music there, gaining some success but continually looking for greater opportunities.

Tech & AI Innovation (2025-Present)

2025 - Present

Starting in 2025, I noticed how many advancements were being made in AI. I decided to bring the world of quantitative data to me by creating Meow Terminal.

Meow Terminal uses financial algorithms, machine learning, and the Black-Scholes model to provide predictive price forecasts on assets with a confidence interval.

I was contacted by the founder of ModeNetwork to talk about predictive modeling for financial assets, and I'm still working on this project currently. I even implemented my own creative ideas such as the 3D calendar tower graph that helps users spot trends on each trading day of the month.

"Most of all my greatest accomplishment is that I'm still standing, I'm healthy and happy. That's what's most important in life to me."
